Boracay Island

Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines. It is famous for its beautiful white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can enjoy various water activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and kiteboarding. The island also offers vibrant nightlife with beachfront bars and restaurants.

Puerto Princesa Underground River

The Puerto Princesa Underground River is located in Palawan, Philippines. It is one of the New7Wonders of Nature and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visitors can take a boat tour through the underground river, marveling at its limestone formations and diverse ecosystem.

Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills are a geological formation in Bohol, Philippines. They consist of over 1,200 hills covered in green grass that turns brown during the dry season, giving them the appearance of chocolate kisses. The Chocolate Hills are a popular tourist destination and a must-visit for nature lovers.

Taal Volcano

Taal Volcano is a complex volcano located on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. It is known for its picturesque crater lake, which contains another small volcanic island called Vulcan Point. Visitors can take a boat ride to the crater lake and hike to the rim for stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
